# What?

---

### Git is revision control software.

- Distributed VCS
- History tracking
- Automatic merging
- Bisecting bugs

---

### History

- Written by Linus Torvalds in 2005 to meet the needs of the Linux kernel developers.

- Now maintained by [Junio Hamano](http://git-blame.blogspot.com/).
